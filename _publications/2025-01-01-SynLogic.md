This paper introduces SynLogic, a scalable framework for synthesizing verifiable logical reasoning data that helps improve the logical reasoning capabilities of large language models. The approach generates high-quality, annotated reasoning examples at scale, enabling better training and evaluation of LLM reasoning abilities.
